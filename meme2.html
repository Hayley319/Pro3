Some of Y’all Lack

Date: April 15, 2025
Author: @cornyloser
Tags: Internet culture, Stan Twitter, humor, pop culture

“Some of Y’all Lack” is a meme format that pairs the phrase with images of celebrities or items, implying that the audience lacks appreciation or understanding. It went viral when @cornyloser tweeted a mashup of an album cover and French fries, captioned “Some of y’all lack.” Users quickly made their own versions to highlight underappreciated figures or items.

Date: April 23, 2025
Author: University of South Carolina's MIND Club
Tags: Mental Health, Social Media, Youth Advocacy, Viral Challenge

University of South Carolina's MIND Club relaunched the Ice Bucket Challenge to promote youth mental health. The viral trend helped raise over $340,000 for Active Minds. It was supported by celebrities and adapted into broader mental health advocacy.
